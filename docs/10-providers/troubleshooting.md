## Common issues

## Lite won’t connect to Hub

- Confirm you’re using the latest app version from the official stores.
- Re-try the QR scan flow and ensure the camera permission is granted.
- If you’re on a restrictive network, test on a different Wi‑Fi / hotspot.

Official references:

- Hub: `https://hub.acurast.com/`
- Processor overview: `https://docs.acurast.com/acurast-processors/`
- Provider onboarding: `https://docs.acurast.com/processors/become-compute-provider/`

## Core setup fails after factory reset

- Confirm **Android 12+**, device is **not rooted**, and bootloader is **locked**.
- Re-run the official setup steps and ensure the device is freshly reset.

Reference: `https://docs.acurast.com/acurast-processors/`
