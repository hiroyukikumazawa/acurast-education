## What “Staked Compute” is

Staked Compute is Acurast’s staking mechanism designed to economically secure reliable compute capacity.

From the official overview:

- **Committers (compute providers)** stake ACU as collateral to back capacity commitments and can be **slashed** if they fail to provide reliable availability.
- **Delegators** stake by delegating to committers; they can earn rewards but share slashing risk.

## Why it matters

- Providers and delegators both have “skin in the game”.
- Developers get more predictable capacity they can build on.
