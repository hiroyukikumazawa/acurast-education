## Official source (primary)

Everything in this section is based on the official Acurast Tokenomics page:
`https://docs.acurast.com/tokenomics`

## Key highlights (from official docs)

- **Initial supply**: 1,000,000,000 ACU  
- **Fixed inflation**: 5% annual inflation with distribution:
  - 70% → Staked Compute Pool
  - 10% → Compute Pool (benchmark rewards)
  - 15% → On-chain treasury
  - 5% → Collators
- **Community-first allocation**: nearly 70% allocated to community or community-supporting purposes

## Token utility (from official docs)

- Governance
- Staking (Staked Compute)
- Incentivization of compute provision
- Network fees (gas)
