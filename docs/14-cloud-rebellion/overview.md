## What this is

Cloud Rebellion is an onboarding + quest experience around Acurast, where you can complete tasks (like connecting Processor Lite to the Hub or deploying your first app) and earn points/rewards depending on the program phase.

## Official references (primary)

- Cloud Rebellion site: `https://rebellion.acurast.com/`
- Acurast Hub Rebellion page: `https://hub.acurast.com/rebellion`
- Tutorial: Connect Lite → Hub + Cloud Rebellion: `https://acurast.com/blog/tutorials/connect-acurast-lite-hub-cloud-rebellion/`
- “Deploy first app (Hub)” notes a Rebellion quest: `https://docs.acurast.com/developers/deploy-first-app-console`

## Common quests (examples)

- Connect Processor Lite to Hub
- Deploy your first app (via Hub or CLI)
- Keep a processor online and available
