# Community Kit (complete)

This kit is a “community-in-a-box” set of templates and playbooks to run workshops, meetups, content sprints, and support channels for Acurast education.

## What’s included

- **Meetup-in-a-box**: agenda, run-of-show, checklists, feedback form template
- **Workshop kits**: 60/90/120 minute facilitation guides + labs
- **Content sprint kit**: how to plan and ship community content in 1–2 hours
- **Creator templates**: video scripts, blog post outlines, slide deck skeletons
- **Community ops**: moderation and support playbook, triage, escalation
- **Localization**: translation workflow and glossary approach
